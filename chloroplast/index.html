The Chloroplast

What is the Chloroplast?

The Chloroplast is an orgenelle that is in plant cells. Chloroplasts are responsible for acquiring energy from sunlight with the assistance of Chlorophyll. The chloroplast is usually in all Plant Cells that photosynthesize. The formula for photosynthesis is 6CO₂ + sunlight + 6H₂O yields C₆H₁₂O₆ + 6O₂. The "+ 6O₂" is the waste product of photosynthesis, Oxygen. Plants have some left over oxygen whenever they finish photosynthesis.
